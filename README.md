# Introduction
In Dickerson Middle School I participated in Science Fair. I was in sixth grade and I wanted to create something that can solve the daily problems of a common person.

## Problem Statement
What problem are you going to solve?  
The problem that I was planning to solve is that they are many different ways to get energy, but some of them are not good for the environment or just are not good for all weathers and seasons. 

## Solution
I combined solar energy and wind energy to create a device that generates a lot of power, it is good for the environment, and is usable all year. I made a small scaled version of a solar-panel and a wind-mill combined.
